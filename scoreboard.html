    Christmas Quiz - How To Play

    This game is played with your smartphone or tablet and the Heritage Window (the large screen at the back of Blackwell Hall).

    To play, open your web browser (we suggest you connect to WiFi) and go to:

    bit.ly / bodxmas

    As each question comes up on the Heritage Window, press the corresponding button on your phone to answer it - A, B, C, or D.

    Your score is your longest consecutive streak of correct answers. Scores appear on scoreboards around the room. Drop in or out whenever you like, and see who's got the highest score at the end of the party!
